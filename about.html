Eugenia Koo

Eugenia loves the smell of a good sell. After completing an undergraduate degree with honors in Finance and International Business, Eugenia grabbed Wall Street by the horns with an Investment Banking career covering financial institutions clients at Bank of America Merrill Lynch.

During her second year her curiosity around early stage ventures seduced her free moments; she joined the Lean Startup Machine team part-time as their "Jill of All Trades" (also known as "Director of Business Development") to drive local partnership and hiring efforts. Her network and interest in bolstering female participation in the New York City tech arena led Eugenia to help organize Hackn' Jill.

This summer, Eugenia will be serving as Venture Acceleration Associate for kbs+p Ventures to examine operational models for their advertising and marketing technology portfolio companies. Eugenia is additionally exploring entrepreneurial opportunities around professional sports news content.
